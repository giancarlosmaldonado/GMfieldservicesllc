Property Preservation Services

Comprehensive Property Preservation Solutions

GM Field Services LLC provides full-scale property preservation services to maintain and protect your assets. We ensure that properties remain compliant with industry standards and are secure, clean, and well-maintained.

Our services include:

  • Securing the Property: Lock changes, boarding windows/doors.
  • Debris Removal: Interior and exterior debris clearance.
  • Lawn Maintenance: Lawn mowing, tree and shrub trimming.
  • Winterization: Protecting properties against cold weather.
  • Snow Removal: Clearing driveways and walkways.
  • Roof Repair & Gutter Cleaning: Ensuring the integrity of the property.
  • Pool Securing & Maintenance: Covering and treating pools.
  • Pest Control: Extermination of rodents, termites, and more.
  • Health & Safety Repairs: Basic electrical and plumbing fixes.
  • Plumbing & Electrical Maintenance: Service reactivations.
  • Janitorial Services: Interior cleaning and maid services.
  • Property Inspections: Regular assessments to ensure compliance.

We are dedicated to protecting your property with **top-tier preservation services** that comply with **all industry regulations**.
