Property Maintenance

Expert Property Maintenance Services

At **GM Field Services LLC**, our team of **highly trained professionals** is dedicated to providing top-tier property maintenance services. We ensure that properties are kept in **optimal condition**, following the highest industry standards set by **Fannie Mae and Freddie Mac**.

Our property maintenance services include:

  • Lock Changes & Securing: Ensuring vacant properties are safe and compliant.
  • Boarding & Window Repairs: Securing broken windows and access points.
  • Debris Removal: Clearing interior and exterior debris, including hazardous materials.
  • Lawn Maintenance: Regular lawn mowing, shrub trimming, and tree care.
  • Winterization: Protecting plumbing and heating systems from extreme weather.
  • Snow Removal: Clearing snow from driveways, walkways, and entrances.
  • Roof Repair & Gutter Cleaning: Ensuring the structural integrity of the property.
  • Pool Securing & Maintenance: Keeping pools safe and in compliance with regulations.
  • Pest Control Services: Eliminating rodents, termites, and insects.
  • Health & Safety Repairs: Electrical, plumbing, and structural fixes.
  • Plumbing & Electrical Systems: Reactivating and maintaining essential utilities.
  • Janitorial & Cleaning Services: Ensuring homes are clean and market-ready.
  • Routine Property Inspections: Comprehensive reports to maintain compliance.

We are **committed to excellence** in every aspect of property maintenance. Whether you need **routine upkeep** or **emergency repairs**, GM Field Services LLC has you covered.
